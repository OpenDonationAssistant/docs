**News Service** acts as a communication bridge between the ODA platform and its users (streamers). It manages:

- **Global News:** Creation and retrieval of platform-wide announcements.
- **Personalized Feeds:** Tracking the "read" state for individual streamers to ensure they see new content in sequence.
- **User Feedback:** Allowing streamers to rate news items, providing data for platform improvement.
